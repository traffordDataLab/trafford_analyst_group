Trafford analyst group

Aiming to bring together analysts of all levels with different areas of expertise across Trafford Council and potentially beyond, to exchange tips, skills and ideas for the benefit of all.

This webpage is intended to provide access to the workshop materials on GitHub. as well as to provide a learning tool for improving web skills, similar in concept to CSS Zen Garden.

Workshop 1: Benefits of CSV

Date: 2019-03-11

Overview: How we structure our data and the format we use to store it can determine its versatility and usefulness to others (reproducibility). This workshop explains the benefits of using the CSV (Comma Separated Values) format and demonstrates how Excel users can use it to separate their data from the presentation whilst still making use of the spreadsheet features.

The following materials are available to download:

  • Sample dataset: speed_of_animals_abridged.csv
  • Spreadsheet connected to the data: speed_of_animals_analysis.xlsx
  • Step-by-step guide to linking an Excel spreadsheet to an external CSV: linking_csv_data_to_excel.txt
